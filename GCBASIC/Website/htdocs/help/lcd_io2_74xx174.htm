See LCD_IO  2_74xx164 for the preferred method to connect an LCD display to a microcomputer via a shift register.


LCD_IO 2_74xx174 has been deprecated (at August 2015) method mode to connect an LCD display to a microcomputer via a shift register either a 74LS174 (or a 74LS164 with diode connected to pin 11).   This method does not support backlight control and has no additional input/output pin.


This is identical to LCD_IO 2 and is included to show the two methods of connecting via a shift register.
