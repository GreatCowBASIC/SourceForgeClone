LCDBacklight
 Previous  Next
Syntax:    
    LCDBacklight ( On | Off )

Command Availability:
Available on all microcontrollers

Explanation:
Sets the LCD backlight  on or off

Do not connect the LCD backlight  to the microprocessor!  Always refer to the datasheet for the correct method to drive the LCD backlight.
